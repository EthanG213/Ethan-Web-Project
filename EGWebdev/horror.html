I love all things horror. I think it is a very interesting genre that can reflect our society and innermost deepest thoughts and fears.

Here is some horror media I enjoy

Cry of Fear this game is a classic to me, and is my favorite horror game for it's dark story and themes.
Resident Evil 2 Remake This game is also very great. Another classic in the Resident Evil franchise I enjoy.
Dead by Daylight Dead by Daylight is a favorite horror game of mine for its fun gameplay, interesting characters and crossovers with other horror franchises.
GTFO GTFO is a intense game with teamwork at its core gameplay. As the title says, "work together or die together".
Silent Hill 2 While I have played the original, the remake of it coming out next year will be very exciting to play as I think Silent Hill is one of the best horror games of all time.
Silent Hill 3 Silent Hill 3 is one of the last games that the original Team Silent worked on before splitting apart. I think the role of Heather as the main character was a great choice, as a teen girl traveling to Silent Hill and being the adopted daughter of Harry Mason makes her a resilent character.
